Concise roadmap for teaching Seaborn:

### **Week 3: Seaborn Fundamentals**
- Why Seaborn? (statistical visualization)
- Distribution plots: `histplot`, `kdeplot`, `boxplot`, `violinplot`
- Categorical plots: `barplot`, `countplot`, `stripplot`
- Seaborn themes and color palettes

### **Week 4: Advanced Visualizations**
- Relationship plots: `scatterplot`, `lineplot`, `regplot`
- Matrix plots: `heatmap`, `clustermap`
- Multi-plot grids: `FacetGrid`, `PairGrid`
- Combining Matplotlib + Seaborn

**Focus:** 70% hands-on coding, 30% theory. Each week includes 2-3 mini-projects using real datasets.
